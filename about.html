WELCOME TO RAM STUDIO

Ram studio is a subsidiary of CSIT 208 GROUP 1 a multi-faceted business organization that provides professional photographic and digital imaging services to corporate clients and individuals from all walks of life. From humble beginnings as a portrait photography studio in 1991 in ACCRA GHANA, RAM STUDIO has expanded to having over thirty (30) Outlets across the 16 regions in Ghana after close to three decades in operation.Having ventured into other aspects of photography such as Wedding Photography, Fashion Photography, Baby Photography, Events Photography, Commercial Photography and School Photography amongst others; the RAM STUDIO brand stands to represent quality imagery in all ramifications.

With a strong reputation for providing quality products with excellent customer care services, RAM STUDIO satisfies over 200,000 clients annually, and the figures continue to grow. Notwithstanding, the company is striving to be even better than previous, because as the saying goes “You are only as good as your last customer”. This is why our customers continue to come to RAM STUDIO year after year to have lasting memories of themselves and their loved ones.

OUR VISION

RAM SUDIO is dedicated to becoming the “Global Leader” in retail imaging and amongst the top ten brands in the global creative industry (audio, visual and entertainment)




OUR MISSION

RAM SUDIO is to be one-stop-shop, providing the whole gamut services from image capture to delivery (Crafting the world’s favorite retail imaging brand via a fusion of creative talent, technology, and commercial know-how, the RAM SUDIO brand aspire to provide affordable world-class photographic and digital imaging services to the growing population of consumers. We aim to establish year on year increase in market share in each of our business locations by offering a superior level of customer service with competitive pricing policies together with continuous employee training and development. As we create our brand as a technology-driven multi-faceted business, the RAM SUDIO enterprise, in addition, operates in every major city via close-knit franchise partnerships thereby creating wealth and securing employment for our shareholders and employees respectively. Our investments span into segments of the tourism, imaging and energy industries.
